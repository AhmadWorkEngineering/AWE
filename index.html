Introduction

Chairman Nusrat Ali, graduated from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore commenced Ahmed Engineering works in 2003. He began his carrier in 1977 as an internee in Shakarkang Mills and resigned in 2003 from the company as the General Manager (Engineering).

Right after the resignation he started his own company, soon enough the company was well known and its current operations are spread nationally.

Since the past few years there has been a drastic change in technology and advancing in our equipment has been our hobby for the development of best quality products. We provide solution that are cost effective and best for the situation. Our devotion for your satisfaction is such that we produce some of the products such as cranes and lubrication pumps that meet your requirements exactly. We are working on to start up or own workshop in the coming future.
